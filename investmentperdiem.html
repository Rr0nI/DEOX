Lucrative offers for new customers: Secure a long interest rate guarantee for call money

In contrast to the often vanishingly small, variable interest rates for existing customers, you can achieve significantly higher returns with special call money campaign interest rates for new customers . It is important that the bank guarantees you this high interest rate for new customers for as long as possible. As long as the interest guarantee applies, your call money is protected from interest rate changes. Should you only receive minimal interest from the bank after this guarantee has expired, you can easily switch back to the best call money account with higher interest rates. Here, another look at the CHECK24 money market account comparison helps to find the bank with the best conditions. With the CHECK24 investment account, it is also particularly easy to change overnight money.

Highest level of security: pay attention to country credit ratings for call money offers

Undoubtedly, one of the biggest advantages of call money is that your investment is protected by law throughout the EU up to the upper limit of one hundred thousand euros. In the CHECK24 overnight money comparison, you will also find the creditworthiness rating of the country in which the bank has its registered office in the details of each offer . Based on the rating of the rating agency Standard & Poor's, you can immediately see where the solvency of the relevant country is between the very good "AAA" and the lowest rating "D". CHECK24 attaches great importance to the security of your overnight money investments . That is why in the CHECK24 daily money comparison you will only find providers from countries that have at least a rating of BB-.

Best interest rates: invest overnight money abroad

CHECK24 gives you an overview of the best call money rates from many banks across Europe at a glance . All offers are also open to you as a German saver. However, when comparing overnight money, investors should not only pay attention to the best interest rates, but also to the security of their overnight money. The statutory deposit insurance also secures your savings in other European countries at any bank up to an amount of 100,000 euros. When comparing the money market account, note that the money houses pay out your interest income at different intervals. For example, a 14-day interest credit gives you quicker access to your interest income than an annual interest credit. In addition, a faster payout can result in compound interest - provided the income is credited directly to your call money account.

Overnight money in foreign currency: possible risks

Within the EU, overnight money is also protected by the European deposit insurance in countries that do not have the euro as their national currency. Instead of the security limit of 100,000 euros, a corresponding value in the respective foreign currency that comes as close as possible to the euro amount applies here. This value changes depending on the current exchange ratethe foreign currency. If the statutory deposit insurance applies, the protected deposits are repaid in the local currency, the exchange rate of which, like any currency, can be subject to fluctuations. Banks from outside the EU that do not have a European subsidiary or a branch in an EU country, however, cannot have any of the local ones join the deposit insurance fund. As a result, the risk for savers at such institutions is disproportionately higher. In the worst case, fluctuating exchange rates can also lead to a negative return on overnight money.
