INTERNET AND TV

If you're looking for entertainment, you don't need an antenna or satellite dish. Thanks to the extensive TV range of many Internet providers, films, series, documentaries, news and sporting events can be delivered to your home via the Internet connection. You can find out how to combine internet and TV with Triple Play on this guide page.

In addition to the Internet provider, you can of course also watch TV on the Internet using TV apps such as Zattoo or waipu.tv or video streaming services such as Netflix, Prime Video or Disney+.

All from a single source

Internet, TV and landline come in a complete package from one provider. Streaming content is included in many Internet and TV tariffs and thanks to your own TV apps you can also watch all content on the go.

Large variety of programs

Secure numerous TV channels with a complete internet and television package. You can watch many TV channels in HD at no extra cost. Thanks to the right hardware, you can even customize your TV program.

Guaranteed cheaper apps

Even complete packages with Internet, TV and landline are guaranteed to be cheaper with CHECK24 than with the provider directly - we promise you that. CHECK24 donates up to €370 cashback on Internet tariffs.

Depending on whether you use DSL, fiber optics or cable for your Internet connection, there are subtle differences with Triple Play. If you want to use Triple Play via a cable connection from Vodafone or PŸUR, you have to make sure, for example, that you already have an active cable connection.

With an active TV cable connection, you already have a basic TV service. If you have a TV cable jack but have not yet activated the cable connection, you must do so before you can use Triple Play. The activation of the cable connection can be easily ordered together with the tariff.

With a DSL or fiber optic connection, you can of course also book a tariff for Internet and television . With such a complete package, you can enjoy the best TV entertainment with exclusive streaming offers, just like with a complete cable package for Internet and television.

Streaming via Netflix & Co.

Series and film highlights on demand, at home or on the go, can be found with streaming providers such as Netflix, Disney+ or Prime Video.
